Backlit Fabric Prints (SEG Lightboxes)

Upgrade from traditional sunboard graphics to premium fabric lightboxes. Silicone Edge Graphics (SEG) use high-resolution dye-sublimation or UV print on stretch polyester fabric, locked into thin aluminum channels. This provides a completely borderless, glare-free, and upscale canvas that is easy to replace.

Print Technology
Dye-Sublimation, Direct UV printing
Fabric Material
German Premium Knit Stretch Polyester
Frame Profile
15mm to 120mm aluminium extrusions
Graphic Replacement
User-swappable in 5 minutes (no tools)
